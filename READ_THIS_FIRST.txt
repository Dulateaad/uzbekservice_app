Step 1: Fix Permissions
-----------------------
Run this script (requires password):

    cd ~/uzbekservice_app
    ./RESTART_MAC_FIX.sh

Or manually:
    sudo chown -R $(whoami) ~/Library/Developer/Xcode/DerivedData/
    sudo chmod -R u+w ~/Library/Developer/Xcode/DerivedData/


Step 2: Verify Full Disk Access
--------------------------------
1. System Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Full Disk Access
2. Make sure Xcode toggle is ON (blue)
3. If you just enabled it, you MUST restart Mac!


Step 3: RESTART YOUR MAC
-------------------------
This is CRITICAL! After enabling Full Disk Access, you MUST restart
your Mac for the permissions to take effect.


Step 4: After Restart, Try Building
------------------------------------
    cd ~/uzbekservice_app
    flutter clean
    flutter pub get
    flutter build ipa --release
